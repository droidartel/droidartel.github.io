Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 2023

This Privacy Policy describes how Droid Artel ("we", "us", or "our") handles information in the Term mobile application for Android.

1. Overview

Term is a terminal emulator. We are committed to protecting your privacy. The application is designed to function without collecting or transmitting your personal data to our own servers. We do not own or operate any servers for storing user information.

2. Data Collection and Usage

The application itself does not collect personal identifiers, contact lists, location data, or files stored on your device. However, to improve performance, debug errors, and support development through advertising, we use third-party SDKs.

Third-Party Services

These services may collect technical information (such as device ID, IP address, OS version, and app usage statistics):

  • Yandex AppMetrica: Analytics and error reporting. Privacy Policy
  • MyTracker: Analytical tracking. Privacy Policy
  • Tracer: App performance monitoring and debugging. Website
  • Yandex Advertising Network: Ad delivery. Privacy Policy

3. Open Source Libraries

The application utilizes the following libraries to provide secure terminal functionality (SSH/Encryption):

  • JSch: For SSH2 implementation.
  • Bouncy Castle: For cryptography and encryption.

These libraries do not collect user data but are essential for the app's technical operations.

4. Permissions

The app may request access to the Internet (to establish terminal connections) and Storage (to save/load configuration files). These permissions are used strictly for the app's core functionality.

5. Security

We value your trust in using our application. Since we do not store your data on our servers, the security of your terminal sessions depends on the protocols provided by the libraries (JSch, Bouncy Castle) and your network environment.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.
